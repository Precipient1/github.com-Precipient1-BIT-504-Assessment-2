About Haukai Restaurant

Haukai Restaurant was founded in 2015 by a husband and wife team who wanted to bring a taste of their native Hawaii to their new home. Since opening, we have become a beloved community gathering place, known for our warm atmosphere and delicious food. Our menu features a variety of dishes inspired by traditional Hawaiian cuisine, as well as a selection of classic American dishes. We pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients and making everything from scratch. Our team is dedicated to providing excellent customer service and a memorable dining experience for all of our guests.
